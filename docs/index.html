How To Play:

  • Cards are laid down in rows face down.
  • Two cards can be turn over per turn.
  • If cards match, cards remain face up.
  • If cards do not match, cards turn face down again.
  • During every turn, once two cards have been selected, "move points" will increase.
  • Match all cards to its pair with the least "move points" to win.

Don't forget to have fun!
